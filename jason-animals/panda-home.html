The Panda

The panda, also known as the giant panda or the panda bear, is a bear species native to China.
Pandas are mostly herbivorous, with a diet consisting almost entirely of bamboo and bamboo shoots.
A panda's white markings help it camoflage into snow, and it's black markings help it camoflage into shade.
They used to be considered an endangered species, but since 2021 have been graded as a "vulnerable" species.
