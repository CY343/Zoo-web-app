The Red Panda

The red panda, also known as the lesser panda, is a small mammal native to the eastern Himalayas and southwestern China.
Red pandas feed mainly on bamboo shoots and leaves, but also on fruits and blossoms.
Despite the name resemblance, red pandas are not related giant pandas.
A red panda's genetics much closely resembles animals of the Mustelidae family, which includes weasels and otters.
Despite not being related to giant pandas., their patterns serve the same function, to camoflage.
