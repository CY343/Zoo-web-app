Introducing the zoo

Welcome to Mad ZOO, a place where adventure, curiosity, and wildlife come together! Our zoo features a diverse collection of fascinating animals from around the world, each with its own unique story and charm. Visitors can explore the mysterious Black Mamba, one of the most agile and feared snakes on the planet, or marvel at the beauty of the White Foxes with their elegant, bushy tails. For dog lovers, our playful Biewer Terriers are sure to bring a smile, while the regal Persian Cats show off their luxurious coats and graceful demeanor. Our exotic mammals section includes adorable Pandas, energetic Red Pandas, clever Raccoons, and the fascinating Common Raccoon Dogs, giving guests a glimpse into the lives of some of the most unique creatures on Earth. For those who love the skies, our bird aviary is home to majestic Condors, powerful Eagles, speedy Falcons, and impressive Vultures, each demonstrating the beauty and skill of the avian world. At Mad ZOO, every corner tells a story, every animal sparks curiosity, and every visit is an unforgettable adventure. Come explore, learn, and connect with the amazing creatures that make our planet so extraordinary!
